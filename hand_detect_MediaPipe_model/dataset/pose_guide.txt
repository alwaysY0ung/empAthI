본 폴더의 모든 8종류 제스처 학습데이터는 정소윤(SoyunJung)이 직접 촬영하여 제작하였습니다.

각 포즈 안내
capture: 검지와 엄지를 피고 해당하는 두 손가락이 90도를 이룬 포즈.
good: 주먹에서 엄지만 펼친 포즈.
okay: 검지와 엄지를 동그랗게 붙이고, 나머지 세 손가락을 자연스럽게 펼친 포즈.
back: 다섯 손가락 모두 펼친 포즈.
spin: 검지 손가락만 펼친 포즈.
stop: 주먹 쥔 포즈.
click: 검지와 중지만 펼친 포즈. 일명 Victory 포즈.
away: 다섯 손가락을 늘어뜨린 포즈.

========
All eight types of gesture training data in this folder were personally filmed and created by Soyun Jung.

Description of each pose:

capture: A pose where the index finger and thumb are extended, forming a 90-degree angle between them.
good: A pose where the fist is made, and only the thumb is extended.
okay: A pose where the index finger and thumb form a circle, with the other three fingers naturally extended.
back: A pose where all five fingers are extended.
spin: A pose where only the index finger is extended.
stop: A pose with a clenched fist.
click: A pose where the index and middle fingers are extended, also known as the Victory pose.
away: A pose where all five fingers are relaxed and hanging down.
